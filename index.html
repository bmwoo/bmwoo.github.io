About Me

I'm a second-year graduate student in psychology and a Frank Knox Memorial Fellow at Harvard University.

My research focuses on mental state understanding, moral cognition, and how mind perception influences moral evaluation and decision-making. I do this research with both child and adult populations. Here at Harvard, I conduct research with Dr. Jason Mitchell and Dr. Elizabeth Spelke.

I received my BSc in Psychology from the University of British Columbia (UBC) in May 2017. At UBC, I completed my senior undergraduate honors thesis with Dr. Kiley Hamlin. Our research has been published in Cognition.
